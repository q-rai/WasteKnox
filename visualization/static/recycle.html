What can I recycle?

Please recycle me:

  • Plastic containers #1-7
    • Please take plastic bags back to your local grocery store for recycling
  • Metals:
    • Aluminum cans
    • Steel Cans
    • Tin Cans
  • Mixed Paper: Any type of 100% paper product can be recycled, including
    • Phone Books
    • Hard & Soft bound books
    • White & Colored paper and stationary
    • Copy paper
    • Sticky notes
    • Envelopes, with or without plastic windows
    • Manilla and hanging folders (Please remove metal bands)
    • Paperboard, including paper towel/toilet paper roll tubes
    • Cereal and tissue boxes
    • Shredded paper
    • Paper bags
  • Clear Glass
  • Brown Glass
  • Cardboard (Please flatten me!)
  • Newspaper
  • Reusable household goods (If Goodwill attendent present)

I go in the trash:

  • Styrofoam
  • Plastic bags and films
  • Batteries
    • Rechargable must be taken to Solid Waste Management Facility)
    • County and UT Campus recycling centers also have battery recycling options
  • Styrafoam
  • Aluminum foil, foil pans & plates
  • Bags/Wrappers with silver linings (chips,candy,etc)
  • Aerosol spray cans/paint cans
  • Pet food bags
  • Cords & Garden hoses
  • Light bulbs and holiday lights
  • Waxed paper or waxed cardboard containers (Milk cartons, etc)
  • Window glass/ Broken Windshields
  • Porcelain or pottery material, plates, etc
  • Plastic containers with toxic waste such as pesticides/herbicides, motor oil, antifreeze
  • Carbon Paper (reciepts, etc)
  • Scrap Metal of any sort
    • Check for a local scrap metal recycler
  • Medical Waste, Insulin sharps, sharps containers
    • Please put these inside plastic containers in your regular trash
    • Your disposal management company may have further instructions
